WHO WE ARE

A SANCTUARY IN NATURE

Nestled in the heart of lush green landscapes near the renowned Nagzira Wildlife Sanctuary, Bhimson's Agro Park & Tourism is a sanctuary for those seeking a rejuvenating escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Spread across a sprawling 7-acre campus in Navargoan, Goregaon (Gondia district), we offer a unique blend of agro-tourism, adventure activities, and eco-friendly living. Our park is designed to bring you closer to nature while delivering heart-pumping thrills and authentic rural experiences.

From the adrenaline rush of wall climbing and jungle safaris to the peaceful serenity of bird watching by a beautiful nearby lake, every moment at Bhimson's is crafted to help you feel yourself and heal yourself.

WHAT WE OFFER

EXPERIENCES THAT MATTER

Adventure Activities

Over 20 thrilling activities including wall climbing, Burma bridge, camping, and more. Designed for all ages and thrill levels.

Jungle Safari

Explore the wild near Nagzira Wildlife Sanctuary. Spot exotic birds, wildlife, and immerse yourself in the untouched beauty of nature.

Camping

Sleep under the stars in nature. Our camping setup lets you disconnect from the city and reconnect with the great outdoors.

Authentic Desi Food

Savour authentic Vidarbha cuisine — from the famous Laal Bhaji and Lasun Pithla to farm-fresh meals prepared with love and tradition.

Farm Experiences

Get hands-on with rural life — organic farming, animal interaction, and traditional agricultural activities that reconnect you with the earth.

Comfortable Stay

8 deluxe rooms designed for comfort amidst nature. Perfect for weekend getaways with 2-night, 3-day packages for couples and families.
